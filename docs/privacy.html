Privacy Policy

My PasswordVault Pro

Last Updated: December 18, 2025

Your privacy is our top priority. My PasswordVault Pro is designed with a privacy-first approach. Your passwords and sensitive data are stored locally on your device and are never transmitted to our servers.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information. All data you enter into My PasswordVault Pro, including:

  • Passwords and login credentials
  • Secure notes
  • Credit card information
  • Any other sensitive data

...is stored exclusively on your device using Apple's secure iOS Keychain with AES-256-GCM encryption.

2. Data Storage & Security

Your data is protected using industry-leading security measures:

  • iOS Keychain: All sensitive data is stored in Apple's hardware-backed Secure Enclave
  • AES-256-GCM Encryption: Military-grade encryption for all stored data
  • Local Storage Only: Your data never leaves your device
  • Biometric Protection: Optional Face ID/Touch ID authentication

3. Data We Do NOT Collect

We want to be completely transparent. We do NOT collect:

  • Your passwords or login credentials
  • Personal information (name, email, phone)
  • Usage analytics or tracking data
  • Device identifiers
  • Location data
  • Any data for advertising purposes

4. Third-Party Services

My PasswordVault Pro uses the following Apple services:

  • StoreKit: For processing in-app purchases (handled entirely by Apple)
  • AutoFill: iOS Password AutoFill extension (data stays on device)

We do not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

5. In-App Purchases

Premium subscriptions are processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. We do not have access to your payment information. Please refer to Apple's Privacy Policy for information about how Apple handles payment data.

6. Data Deletion

You have complete control over your data:

  • Delete individual passwords, notes, or cards at any time within the app
  • Use the "Delete All Data" option in Settings to remove everything
  • Uninstalling the app will remove all locally stored data

7. Children's Privacy

My PasswordVault Pro is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.
