‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

How do I enable AutoFill?

Go to iPhone Settings ‚Üí Passwords ‚Üí Password Options ‚Üí Turn on AutoFill Passwords ‚Üí Select "My PasswordVault Pro" from the list.

Is my data backed up?

Your passwords are stored in the iOS Keychain, which persists even if you delete and reinstall the app. For additional backup, use the Export feature in Settings to save a CSV file.

How do I import passwords from another app?

Go to Settings ‚Üí Import/Export ‚Üí Import Passwords. We support CSV files from Chrome, Safari, Firefox, 1Password, LastPass, Bitwarden, and more.

What happens if I forget my device passcode?

Your passwords are protected by your device's security. If you reset your device, you'll need to restore from a backup or re-import your passwords from an exported CSV file.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Go to iPhone Settings ‚Üí Your Name ‚Üí Subscriptions ‚Üí My PasswordVault Pro ‚Üí Cancel Subscription. You'll retain premium access until the end of your billing period.

Can I use the app on multiple devices?

Your subscription works on all devices signed into the same Apple ID. However, passwords are stored locally on each device and don't sync automatically between devices.
